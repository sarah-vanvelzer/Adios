# Adios
A SwiftUI app designed to bring closure to food moments through benefit reframing and a visual map.

## About
Adios is an iOS app designed to help people in eating disorder recovery close the loop on food moment.
Instead of tracking calories, or nutrition numbers, the app highlights joy-focused benefits after eating & lets users place the moment on a map.
The goal is simple: acknowledge the moment, take something supportive from it, & let it go.
Each entry becomes a point on the map, a quiet archive of moments that were eaten, lived, & left behind.

## Features
- Benefit-based reframing after eating
- No calorie or nutrition tracking
- Visual map where food moments are placed & released
- Tap entries to revists benefits attached to specific moments

## Built With
SwiftUI
SwiftData
XCode

## Context
Adios was created for the Apple Swift Student Challenge & explores how technology can support people navigating their relationship with food without turning eating into numbers or performance.
It was guided by my own personal experience with an eating disorder.
